About me

I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics in 2024 under the supervision of Dr. Matilde Marcolli. Prior to that I worked under the supervision of Dr. Pavel Winternitz on superintegrable quantum systems. I am currently looking for an opportunity to work in Machine Learning/Data Science. If I am not coding or learning, I am probably lifting some weigths at the gym.
